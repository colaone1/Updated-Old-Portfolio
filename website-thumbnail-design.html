Website Thumbnail Design

Social Media Preview & Brand Enhancement

Role: Lead Designer
Category: Digital Design
Year: 2024

Project Overview

Designed a custom website thumbnail that serves as the social media preview image for portfolio sharing. This design enhances brand visibility and engagement when the portfolio link is shared across platforms like Upwork, LinkedIn, Twitter, and other social networks, creating a professional first impression.

Design Objectives

  • Create an eye-catching social media preview image
  • Enhance brand recognition and professional appearance
  • Optimize for various social media platform requirements
  • Maintain visual clarity at small thumbnail sizes
  • Encourage click-through and engagement
